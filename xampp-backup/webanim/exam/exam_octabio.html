Vision

Stable and self-reliant sugar producing and milling communities. Inorder to improve the economic wellness of the Philippines by providing quality standard for a better and safer sugar production.

CAPABILITIES

SIFI is currently aligning its programs and services to its broader mission and vision. To translate these statements into doable programs, the Board of Trustees has approved a 5-year plan which is known to be the 4 Pillars of Development. Education, Health, Livelihood and Advocacy.

Mission

A professionally run, apolitical, progressive social development umbrella foundation capable of initiating, developing, promoting and funding projects for sugar workers of SIFI members organization, their families and the communities they are part of.
